Succulents

Succulents are plants that have thick, fleshy parts adapted to store water. They are often found in arid climates and are known for their unique appearance.

More about Succulents

Ferns

Ferns are a group of plants that reproduce via spores and have neither seeds nor flowers. They are found in a variety of habitats and are known for their lush, green fronds.

More about Ferns

Orchids

Orchids are a diverse and widespread family of flowering plants with blooms that are often colorful and fragrant. They are popular as ornamental plants and have numerous species.
